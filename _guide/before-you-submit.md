Before anything goes out under your name or the lab's, run this. It gathers the
expectations that live across the guide into one place so nothing gets missed in
the rush before a deadline.

## Always, for any submission

- Give people time. Christina and your co-authors need enough lead time to give real feedback; work that arrives too late can be held back. See [Communication]({{ '/lab-guide/communication/' | relative_url }}).
- Christina has the draft at least two weeks before any external deadline.
- Explicit approval from Christina *and* all co-authors before anything is submitted.
- At least two labmate pre-reviews first (post in **#phone-a-friend** on the lab Slack).
- Disclose honestly: funding, conflicts, data and image permissions, [AI use]({{ '/lab-guide/using-ai/' | relative_url }}), and ethics approvals.
- Every number traces to its source in an annotated script.
{: .checklist}

The first four are the [lab non-negotiables]({{ '/lab-guide/first-drafts/' | relative_url }}#non-negotiables).

## Journal paper or preprint

- Run the [pre-send checklist]({{ '/lab-guide/first-drafts/' | relative_url }}#before-you-send-a-draft-checklist).
- Run the [figure checklist]({{ '/lab-guide/figures/' | relative_url }}#figure-checklist) on every figure.
- Authorship agreed and written down; see [Authorship policy]({{ '/lab-guide/authorship-policy/' | relative_url }}).
- Data and code ready to go public on publication; see [Data management]({{ '/lab-guide/data-management/' | relative_url }}).
- Reference manager used throughout; no typed references.
{: .checklist}

## Conference abstract, paper, poster, or talk

- Get written approval before submitting or booking; see [Conferences]({{ '/lab-guide/conferences/' | relative_url }}).
- Lead with results you can defend; hold exciting findings for the journal version.
- Practice to the lab at least two weeks before you travel; see [Presentations]({{ '/lab-guide/presentations/' | relative_url }}#practice-a-lot).
- For a poster, run the [poster]({{ '/lab-guide/posters/' | relative_url }}) and [figure]({{ '/lab-guide/figures/' | relative_url }}#figure-checklist) checks.
{: .checklist}

## Grant or fellowship

- Give reviewers time, and get several people to read it; see [Funding applications]({{ '/lab-guide/funding-applications/' | relative_url }}).
- Request [recommendation letters]({{ '/lab-guide/recommendation-letters/' | relative_url }}) one to two months ahead.
- Don't use AI to draft scientific text; see [Using AI]({{ '/lab-guide/using-ai/' | relative_url }}).
{: .checklist}

## Booking travel or taking time off

- Get written approval from Christina before you plan or book travel; keep every receipt and register in UC Away. See [Travel]({{ '/lab-guide/travel/' | relative_url }}).
- Log time off with appropriate notice; see [Time off & vacation]({{ '/lab-guide/time-off-vacation/' | relative_url }}).
{: .checklist}
