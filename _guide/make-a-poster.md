## Poster tips

- Include sections for introduction, methods, results, discussion, references, and acknowledgements (they don't all need those exact titles).
- Make box titles informative.
- Keep every sentence short and informative — no fluff words.
- Block off all sections evenly.
- Consider the audience: a poster for a room of experts should look very different from one for the general public.
- Make sure all text is readable, including plot axes and legends.
- Include the logos of the appropriate funding agencies.
- Use the correct color scheme for printing (CMYK).
