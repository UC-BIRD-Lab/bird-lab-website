It is our goal that every member has a fair, equitable, safe, and positive
experience in our community. Use the resources below to seek help for yourself or
someone you know who needs support (with their consent). If you'd like help
getting connected with a confidential resource, just ask.

## Support resources

- [Student Health and Counseling Services](https://shcs.ucdavis.edu/) — confidential (both SHSV and discrimination)
- [Academic and Staff Assistance Program (ASAP)](https://www.hr.ucdavis.edu/departments/asap) — for employees (GSR/TA); confidential
- [Office of the Ombuds](https://ombuds.ucdavis.edu/) — confidential problem-solving and conflict management
- [Center for Advocacy, Resources & Education (CARE)](https://care.ucdavis.edu/) — advocates and a 24/7 help line (confidential for SHSV)
- [LGBTQIA Resource Center](https://lgbtqia.ucdavis.edu/) — community counsellors and wellness workshops
- [Women's Resources and Research Center](https://wrrc.ucdavis.edu/) — centers women, transgender, femme, and non-binary individuals
- [Student Disability Center](https://sdc.ucdavis.edu/) — accommodation requests
- [Explore identities and cultures](https://studentaffairs.ucdavis.edu/student-life/communities/identity) — find and join an affinity group

## Reporting resources

- [Harassment & Discrimination Assistance and Prevention Program (HDAPP)](https://hdapp.ucdavis.edu/)
- [EthicsPoint — University of California](https://secure.ethicspoint.com/domain/media/en/gui/23531/index.html) — report ethical concerns (anonymous option)
- [UC Systemwide Campus Climate](https://ucsystems.ethicspointvp.com/custom/ucs_ccc/default.asp) — report discriminatory situations (anonymous option)
