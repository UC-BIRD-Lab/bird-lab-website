Shipping labels for the lab are created through AggieShip. Labels can be made by
the MAE Front Office or Christina.

## 1. Gather the required information

- Package dimensions (L × W × H) and weight
- Declared value of the item
- Type of packaging (box, padded envelope, tube, manufacturer packaging)
- Manufacturer address, including email and phone number
- Account to charge shipping to
- Delivery speed needed (ground, 2-day, overnight)

## 2. Request the label

Send the information above to the MAE Front Office or Christina; they'll generate
the label in AggieShip and provide it to you.

## 3. Print and attach the label

Print the label and securely attach it to the package, taping all edges so it
won't peel.

## 4. Drop off the package

Take it to the courier drop-off location (most often FedEx). If you're in a rush
and pay out of pocket, follow the [Pay for things]({{ '/lab-guide/pay-for-things/' | relative_url }}) reimbursement process.

<div class="callout callout--warn" markdown="1">
Always get Christina's permission before paying out of pocket for anything.
</div>
