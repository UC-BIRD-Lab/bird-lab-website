**Purpose.** Keep the lab running smoothly with clear, lightweight ownership of
essential operational tasks, so safety, facilities, equipment, data, and
onboarding don't fall through the cracks and we can stay focused on science.

The roles below rotate among lab members. Most take ~10–30 min/week: weekly
(2–5 min) task review, monthly (10–15 min) checklist updates, quarterly
(30–60 min) reviews and walkthroughs, and one annual pass (4–6 hours). Every role
also proposes one small process improvement a year.

<aside class="marginnote" markdown="1">
All students remain responsible for their role in lab safety, even if you are not the lab safety manager.
</aside>

**Where the work lives.** Current assignments, the shared task tracker, and each
role's handoff page are in the [member portal]({{ '/portal/' | relative_url }}).
Each handoff page holds that role's routine and checklists. Roles are reassigned
when a student leaves, and each owner keeps their handoff page current so the next person can
run the role from it alone. When a role gives you authority to
act (pause unsafe work, restrict equipment, freeze access), you have Dr. Harvey's
backing to use it.

<aside class="marginnote marginnote--warn" markdown="1">
No role should require chasing individuals. If someone doesn't comply twice, or
you find yourself chasing external folks, escalate to Christina.
</aside>

**A task is done** when all of its parts are complete. **If something is blocked**
for over a week, or immediately if it's safety- or compliance-critical, tell
Christina what happened, what you need, and by when.
