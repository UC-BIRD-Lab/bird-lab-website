For any presentation where you represent the lab, include the BIRD Lab logo on the
first and last slides. Include UC Davis logos as well — these can be accessed
through the [UC Davis brand guide](https://communicationsguide.ucdavis.edu/brand-guide/logos).

## Logo usage

- **Preferred logo** — use on light backgrounds. Available as PNG and PDF (raptor-head wordmark and a long-format version).
- **Inverted (light) logo** — use on dark backgrounds.
- **Simple mark** — the raptor-head mark without words, for compact uses.

Logo files are kept in the lab's shared brand-assets folder; ask the
Communication & Outreach lead or Christina for the current versions. The site's
own copy of the mark and wordmark lives in `assets/img/` in this repository.
