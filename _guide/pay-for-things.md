## Preferred order of routes

1. **Amazon (when possible)** — fastest, easiest tracking.
2. **OPP / pre-purchasing** — for non-Amazon vendors or special items.
3. **UC Davis Stores** — for items they carry.
4. **Out-of-pocket** — only when approved in advance (rare).

## Amazon orders

Order through Amazon via AggieEnterprise (Christina only) — don't order it
yourself. Submit a request through Slack (Purchase Hub → "Request Amazon
Purchase"). Include exact color/model/size in the item name and justification to
avoid mis-orders.

## OPP (pre-purchasing) for non-Amazon items

Use when an item isn't on Amazon or must come from another vendor. First, ask
Christina to add you to the correct work group. Then submit a request via Slack
(Purchase Hub → "Pre-Purchasing Request"), complete the fields in
[pre-purchasing](https://prepurchasing.ucdavis.edu/), and submit a separate
request per vendor. It routes to Christina for approval.

<div class="callout callout--warn" markdown="1">
Include the URL and any special item codes or offers, otherwise the purchaser
will just buy at full price.
</div>

## UC Davis Stores

A quick option that can use lab accounts directly. Complete the
[departmental purchase](https://ucdavisstores.com/SiteText?id=63390) form and
send it to Christina for the correct account code.

## Urgent / out-of-pocket reimbursement

1. Get written approval from Christina in advance (email or Slack).
2. Preferred: ask Christina to purchase with the lab P-Card.
3. If you must use your own money, **keep your receipts** — without them we cannot reimburse you.
4. Complete the Expense Reimbursement form and email it to Christina for an approval signature **before** continuing. Multiple purchases can go on one form, but the total cannot exceed $500.
5. Create a Payment/Refund order in [pre-purchasing](https://prepurchasing.ucdavis.edu/) (delivery = your address; shipping = "No Shipping Required").
6. In [AggieExpense](https://aggieexpense.ucdavis.edu/), add the lab manager as an expense delegate (ask Christina for the current manager's address), with "Can Prepare" and "Can view receipts" checked.
7. Attach the receipt and completed reimbursement form and submit for approval.

## Tracking & pickup

All purchase requests and pickups are tracked in Slack (Purchase Hub → Tracker
List). When an item arrives it's posted in #purchasing, and whoever picks it up
logs where it was stored.
