Once you've passed the qualifying exam, it's time to think about writing your
dissertation and graduating! Exciting and intimidating times. UC Davis imposes specific requirements on this process; this page outlines what you need to know.

## Keep your committee updated

Your three-person dissertation committee should be kept abreast of your progress
toward your final degree.

## Write your dissertation

Compile your work into a comprehensive document. Note that UC Davis has strict
formatting guidelines; see
[Preparing & filing your thesis or dissertation](https://gradstudies.ucdavis.edu/preparing-filing-your-thesis-or-dissertation).

## File your dissertation

Identify with Christina when you expect to graduate (by quarter). Work backward
from that date:

- **Graduation in 3 quarters:** begin planning milestones with your committee.
- **Graduation in 6 weeks:** finalize the document against the formatting guidelines.
- **Graduation in 4 weeks:** provide your dissertation to your committee.
- **Graduation in 0 weeks:** graduate!
