## One-on-one meetings

- We meet weekly for 30 minutes.
- Be on time. If you can't attend, give at least 24 hours' notice.
- If you expect to need more than half an hour, schedule it with Christina in
  advance. One-off meetings can be arranged over email or Slack.
- Before each meeting, copy the template from the shared Box `MeetingSlides`
  folder and fill in four slides: **Successes**, **Works in progress**, **Things
  to do**, and **Questions**.
- Save your slides in `MeetingSlides` using the naming convention
  `yy_mm_dd_lastname.pptx`.
- After the meeting, return to the slides and add a brief bullet-point recap of
  what you discussed.

## Lab meetings

- Be on time; give at least 24 hours' notice if you can't attend.
- Give presenters your respect and attention. If you must attend to other work,
  take care not to distract others.
- Members present on their research roughly **twice a year**.
- Keep up with literature reviews.
- Share constructive, thoughtful feedback.

## Journal club

We meet every two weeks to discuss a new article, on the weeks without a lab
meeting. See [Journal club]({{ '/lab-guide/journal-club/' | relative_url }}) for
the format and discussion norms.
