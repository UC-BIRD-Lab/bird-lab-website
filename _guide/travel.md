## Booking

Flights, hotel, and registration can go on the lab Travel & Entertainment (T&E)
card, or on your own card to reimburse later.

<aside class="marginnote marginnote--warn" markdown="1">
**Hotel on the T&E card** only if Christina will be there in person. Otherwise,
book it yourself and reimburse.
</aside>

- **Flights** must be the cheapest round-trip from Sacramento.
- **Extending for vacation?** Fine with approval, but any extra flight cost is a
  personal expense.

<aside class="marginnote" markdown="1">
**Traveling internationally?** Extra steps apply (export-control screening,
high-risk-destination approval, visas). See the [member portal]({{ '/portal/' | relative_url }}).
</aside>

UC Davis policy: [lodging](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/travel/lodging),
[ground transport](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/travel/ground-transport),
[meals & incidental expenses](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/travel/meals).

## Register in UC Away

Registering activates UC's travel insurance and emergency assistance.
Out-of-state and international trips must be registered in
[UC Away](https://ehs.ucop.edu/away). This happens automatically if you book
through AggieExpense.


## Receipts & reimbursement

<aside class="marginnote marginnote--warn" markdown="1">
**Keep every receipt** unless Christina has confirmed in writing that you don't
need to.
No receipt, no reimbursement.
</aside>

Photograph each receipt the moment you get it. For how to submit receipts and
file for reimbursement, see the [member portal]({{ '/portal/' | relative_url }}).

**Submit promptly.** UC sets the deadlines, and late reports can become
taxable income; see [taxation of late reports](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/aggieexpense/help/late-reports).
