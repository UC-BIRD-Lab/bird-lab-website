This page covers travel for official lab purposes (not personal vacation). For
time-off logging, see [Time off & vacation]({{ '/lab-guide/vacation/' | relative_url }}),
and for the broader conference workflow see
[Attending a conference]({{ '/lab-guide/attend-a-conference/' | relative_url }}).

## 1. Get approval

Do not plan or book travel without explicit written approval from Christina.

## 2. Book flights, hotel, and registration

These can go on the lab Travel & Entertainment (T&E) card — though the hotel can
only go on the lab card if Christina will be there in person. You may book on your
own card, but flights must be the cheapest round-trip from Sacramento. You may
extend your stay for vacation with approval, but any extra cost from more
expensive flights is a personal expense. See UC Davis policy for
[lodging](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/travel/lodging),
[ground transportation](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/travel/ground-transport),
and [meals & incidental expenses](https://supplychain.ucdavis.edu/travel-entertainment/travel/meals).

## 3. Register your travel

All university-purpose trips must be registered in [UC Away](https://ehs.ucop.edu/away).

## 4. Keep all your receipts

Keep and photograph every receipt immediately to avoid losing them. Anything
without a receipt cannot be reimbursed.

## 5. If the T&E card was used

Send all receipts to Christina — this is non-negotiable; these receipts cannot be
lost.

## 6. File for reimbursement of personal expenses

1. Combine all receipts into a PDF.
2. Fill out the Travel Reimbursement Form.
3. Ask Christina for the appropriate fund to use.
4. Send Christina the completed form to sign.
5. Upload the signed form, receipts, and proof of UC Away registration into the pre-purchasing system. If it's your first time, ask Christina for an example.
