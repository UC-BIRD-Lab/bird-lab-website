{%- comment -%} Slim site-wide announcement bar, rendered just below the header on every page (wired up in _layouts/default.html). All content and the on/off switch live in _data/announcement.yml; edit that file, not this one. Two safety nets keep an expired notice off the live site: 1. Build-time: if the build runs after `deadline` (inclusive), the bar is not rendered at all. 2. Client-side: main.js compares data-deadline against the visitor's clock and removes the bar if the deadline has passed, covering the window between the deadline and the next site rebuild (the site only rebuilds on push, so this net matters).
