**The lab's communication framework.** Every audience asks two fundamental questions: *So what?* and *Who cares?* You answer them by maximizing the signal-to-noise ratio in everything you make. **Signal** is the message you are trying to convey. **Noise** is the stuff standing in your audience's way of understanding. (Source: Jean-Luc Dumont.)
